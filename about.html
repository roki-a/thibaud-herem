ABOUT

Thibaud Herem is a French illustrator, working worldwide, he is known for his distinct signature style, working with pencil and Indian inks. He specialises in creating architectural drawings with an incredible level of hand-drawn detail.

Thibaud has worked with numerous prestigious clients all over the world including Hermes, Four Seasons Hotel, Anya Hindmarch, Conran shop, Hyundai, Samsung, Nike, Arenagirl, Studio Tomboy, and many others. He has reached a new level of excellence by working on the Netflix show "Our Beloved Summer" in 2020.

Thibaud has also worked with private clients, drawing their own favourite historical places.

He has exhibited his work in many galleries all over the world and published several books with Cicada Books (Raising a Forest, Draw me a House), with Nobrow press (London Deco) and with Laurence King Publishing (Story of Trees, Mazes).
